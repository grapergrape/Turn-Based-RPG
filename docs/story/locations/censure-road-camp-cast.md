# Censure Road Camp Voice Cards

These private cards separate the camp's occupational voices. Shared dry humor
is not enough. Each speaker must use a different kind of evidence, pressure,
and authority.

## Preceptor Aquila

- **Background:** Field preceptor responsible for routes, reports, training, and
  the people sent out under her seal.
- **Present pressure:** Turn incomplete chapel and road evidence into an order
  that can survive review by Decian and the east checkpoints.
- **Social tactic:** Forces the listener through a form, then rewards precision
  by removing one practical obstruction.
- **Working vocabulary:** Slate, pencil, form, answer, seal, bell, route, copy.
- **Syntax range:** Form prompts are intentionally clipped and numbered. Spoken
  judgments use longer dry clauses that expose what the form failed to capture.
- **Avoidance:** Scratches out praise before anyone can use it as evidence of
  partiality.
- **Private contradiction:** Mocks paperwork constantly while trusting a good
  record more than any declaration of courage.

## Father Augustine

- **Background:** Road priest who sells field absolution from a tent beside the
  camp's confession screen.
- **Present pressure:** Keep spiritual and financial authority intact while the
  player uncovers his private use of Widow Naomi's need.
- **Social tactic:** Turns confession into a negotiated account, then narrows
  accusations to whatever a witness can prove.
- **Working vocabulary:** Sin, coin, cup, stamp, chit, receipt, ink, margin.
- **Syntax range:** Public speech builds elaborate comparisons between doctrine
  and bookkeeping. When cornered, his grammar becomes exact and legal.
- **Avoidance:** Will not state what Naomi was expected to provide after the last
  bell.
- **Private contradiction:** Claims payment makes sin memorable while relying on
  forgotten, altered, or privately priced records.

## Quartermaster Ruth

- **Background:** Censure issue clerk responsible for rations, ammunition, and
  field packets.
- **Present pressure:** Keep the camp supplied while false weights, forged
  marks, and unofficial trade pass through her ledger.
- **Social tactic:** Makes the listener present the right seal or discrepancy,
  then answers with the exact stock consequence.
- **Working vocabulary:** Issue, seal, beans, packet, weight, return, crate.
- **Syntax range:** Queue speech uses repeated ration formulae by design. Private
  judgments expand into sharp explanations of why a rule failed.
- **Avoidance:** Does not admit how often Judith's unofficial stock keeps the camp
  functional.
- **Private contradiction:** Defends official issue control while depending on a
  trader the Church refuses to name as useful.

## Sutler Judith

- **Background:** Unofficial camp trader who positions her cart inside military
  traffic without belonging to the issue office.
- **Present pressure:** Profit, protect her back room, and remain too useful for
  Aquila or Ruth to remove.
- **Social tactic:** Converts every order, theft, and favor into a price the
  listener can understand.
- **Working vocabulary:** Cart, coin, tally, strip, chalk, price, receipt.
- **Syntax range:** Offers use quick transactional turns. Suspicions use fuller
  sentences that show how closely she reads another trader's hand.
- **Avoidance:** Never inventories the entire back room for an official witness.
- **Private contradiction:** Insists she is merely selling while repeatedly
  performing relief work the official supply chain failed to do.

## Sister Joanna

- **Background:** Censure field medic managing boiled cloth, rejected packets,
  and road casualty records.
- **Present pressure:** Protect usable medicine from contamination and prove
  which wounds or stains belong in Melchior's custody.
- **Social tactic:** Inspects material evidence before accepting the listener's
  account, then amends the medical count.
- **Working vocabulary:** Boil, fold, seal, wax, stain, bandage, wound, basin.
- **Syntax range:** Clinical conclusions connect observation to action. She does
  not borrow Melchior's label fragments or Augustine's metaphors.
- **Avoidance:** Refuses to dramatize suffering while someone still needs care.
- **Private contradiction:** Works inside Censure procedure but writes explicit
  refusals when that procedure would make her carry unsafe evidence.

## Evidence Keeper Melchior

- **Background:** Custodian of cult relics, contaminated packets, and objects
  whose behavior has not settled.
- **Present pressure:** Preserve chain of custody without letting reactive
  evidence spread through the camp.
- **Social tactic:** Reads the tag aloud, contradicts it, then moves the object
  as if correcting an unruly worker.
- **Working vocabulary:** Tag, cord, knot, shelf, sack, corner, distance, fire.
- **Syntax range:** Brief label fragments are an occupational exception reserved
  for quoted classifications. His instructions name a concrete containment act.
- **Avoidance:** Does not speculate about what an object wants while it is still
  within reach.
- **Private contradiction:** Treats evidence as impersonal stock while giving
  each dangerous object the wary attention normally given to a person.

## Brother Cyprian

- **Background:** Drill instructor responsible for baton work, range habits, and
  novice survival.
- **Present pressure:** Correct mistakes before the east road punishes them more
  severely than the yard can.
- **Social tactic:** Demonstrates through the listener's body, then names the
  exact turn or line that failed.
- **Working vocabulary:** Baton, wrist, chalk turn, loop, range, target, guard.
- **Syntax range:** Training commands are short imperatives. Explanation follows
  the completed movement rather than becoming a sermon beforehand.
- **Avoidance:** Does not discuss his own fear or the cost of earlier training
  failures.
- **Private contradiction:** Teaches fear as a usable tool while arranging the
  yard so no novice must discover its use alone.

## Bell Clerk Sabina

- **Background:** Clerk trained to read peal cards, rope wear, and route clauses
  as one signaling system.
- **Present pressure:** Reconstruct an altered return peal without certifying a
  forged witness line.
- **Social tactic:** Places physical records together and makes the mismatch
  visible before offering a conclusion.
- **Working vocabulary:** Peal, rope, splice, strand, rubbing, cut, bell, clause.
- **Syntax range:** Measured technical sentences follow the order of inspection.
  Alarm appears as increased specificity, not a dramatic cadence change.
- **Avoidance:** Does not turn signaling work into theology.
- **Private contradiction:** Trusts exact procedure while proving that someone
  used procedure itself to hide an altered order.

## Writ Runner Philip

- **Background:** Message runner carrying sealed orders between the board,
  chapel, and road staff.
- **Present pressure:** Reconstruct who cut a name or changed a delivery without
  becoming the easiest person to blame.
- **Social tactic:** Repeats the chain of custody in travel order and corrects
  any ambiguous bell or time reference immediately.
- **Working vocabulary:** Tube, wax, seam, name, table, knot, bell, route.
- **Syntax range:** Reports are sequential and careful. Hesitation appears only
  where his attention strayed beyond the official road.
- **Avoidance:** Will not accuse Clerk Tavin without a mark he can carry back.
- **Private contradiction:** Claims to watch roads rather than tents while
  remembering exactly who crossed Augustine's rope after the last bell.

## Ash Porter Baruch

- **Background:** Freight porter who braces road loads and remembers counts in
  cuts on his wrist guard.
- **Present pressure:** Expose a false crate weight without being treated as the
  disposable hand beneath the mistake.
- **Social tactic:** Reconstructs movement with his fingers and lets the physical
  sequence contradict the written tally.
- **Working vocabulary:** Crate, brace, break, weight, mark, axle, destination.
- **Syntax range:** Work sequences compress into ordered verbs. Personal anger
  enters through comparison with the better documented cargo.
- **Avoidance:** Does not name a superior until the load history proves intent.
- **Private contradiction:** Values practical memory over forms while envying
  the destination and identity that paperwork gives a crate.

## Widow Naomi

- **Background:** Camp washer and widow seeking to keep her husband's name in
  the morning office.
- **Present pressure:** Preserve that memorial without letting Augustine define what
  happened after the last bell.
- **Social tactic:** Answers through the order of laundry work, then allows one
  ambiguity to show where certainty would be dangerous.
- **Working vocabulary:** Bandage cloth, shirts, cuffs, wash water, name, bell.
- **Syntax range:** Work speech is direct and complete. Augustine's subject produces
  careful clauses that separate what he promised from what he wanted.
- **Avoidance:** Does not supply a clean label for coercion while she still needs
  the office he controls.
- **Private contradiction:** Understands the priest's appetite clearly but still
  needs his institution to speak her husband's name.

## Tether Guard Justus

- **Background:** South-gate guard responsible for pack animals and the empty
  rope line.
- **Present pressure:** Hold the gate while concealing an embarrassing tether
  failure.
- **Social tactic:** Reports the loss through a joke that admits more than an
  excuse would.
- **Working vocabulary:** Goats, rope, tether, gate, tracks.
- **Syntax range:** One dry reversal is enough. Do not expand him into another
  camp philosopher.
- **Avoidance:** Does not describe the exact moment the goats defeated the line.
- **Private contradiction:** Claims a moral victory because no material victory
  remains to record.

## Novice Isidore

- **Background:** Inexperienced Censure novice drilled under Cyprian.
- **Present pressure:** Appear ready for field work while fear is already
  outpacing his training.
- **Social tactic:** Quotes instruction, then converts his own failure into the
  language of an unwanted job applicant.
- **Working vocabulary:** Fear, tool, sling, drill, promotion, qualification.
- **Syntax range:** His equipment and sentences are overly neat. Nervous humor
  exposes the pressure without turning into a series of quips.
- **Avoidance:** Does not plainly ask to remain in camp.
- **Private contradiction:** Wants promotion in the order while imagining fear
  as the part of him most qualified to receive it.
