# Tileset Prompt

```text
Create an original late-1990s isometric CRPG tileset for [LOCATION]. The tiles must feel like low-resolution pre-rendered or hand-polished isometric environment art: dusty, aged, muted, and readable.

Technical target:
- tile projection: 2:1 isometric diamond
- tile size: 64x32 px or 48x24 px
- internal game resolution: 640x480 or 800x600
- crisp nearest-neighbor pixel edges
- no high-res texture smoothing

Required tiles:
- base floor
- cracked floor
- stained floor
- dusty floor
- broken-corner floor
- blood/damage floor
- burn mark floor
- debris cluster floor
- wall-base transition
- doorway threshold

Texture rules:
- hand-placed grime, not uniform random speckles
- cracks and stains should tell a spatial story
- less contrast than characters
- muted palette: ash gray, dusty brown, dirty beige, rust, dried blood, dull amber light

Wall rules:
- darker than floors
- chipped edges
- vertical grime streaks
- dark wall-floor contact occlusion
- old signage or damage where appropriate

Props to match:
- crates, benches, candles, old machinery, broken chapel/industrial objects
- all props need top/side/front planes, chipped edges, and contact shadows

Negative style:
No clean modern tiles, no glossy PBR, no saturated colors, no random noise blankets, no fantasy JRPG tiles, no copied Fallout maps or assets.
```
